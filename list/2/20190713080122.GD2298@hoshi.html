Hi,

I found meli via reddit and had a quick look at the website - awesome project
and I'd love to contribute a little bit (only a little bit because I have some
other stuff I really have to get going).

I have one question which I did not see as answered after looking at the
website, though:

Do you plan to integrate mail fetching and sending in the mail client itself?
Because I am really in favour of the unix philosophy here and I'd like to see a
mail _reader_ seperate from a mail _sender_ and a mail _fetcher_. Also, I would
love to see integration into other tooling (contact management, calendar
management, task/todo management) _outside_ of the mail client (if this is
applicable at all to your project).


FWIW, you have to note that I am the author of imag [0] the personal information
management suite, where I want to have tools for contact management, mail
tracking (not a MUA/MTA though), todo management, etc etc etc...

What I am mainly asking for is
1) Unix philosophy: One tool for one job
2) Possibility to integrate other tools via scripting (because that whats imag
is focused on).


Feel free to shoot me any questions and get a discussion going!
